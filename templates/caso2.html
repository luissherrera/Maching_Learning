Use Case 2: Student Performance Prediction

Problem Description

Educational institutions often struggle to identify students who are at risk of failing a course. Without early detection, it becomes difficult to provide timely academic support. This use case predicts student performance based on study habits and academic behavior.

Type of Machine Learning

This use case uses Supervised Learning, specifically regression, to predict a student's final performance.

Input Data

  • Study hours per week
  • Attendance percentage
  • Number of assignments completed
  • Previous exam scores

Expected Output

The model predicts the final exam score of the student.

Real-World Relevance

This system helps identify students at academic risk, allowing early intervention, improving academic performance, and supporting personalized learning strategies.
