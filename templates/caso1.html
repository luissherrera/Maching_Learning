Use Case 1: Facial Recognition for Device Unlocking

Problem Description

Users need fast access to their smartphones without compromising security. Traditional methods such as PINs and patterns are vulnerable and slow. A system is required that can identify the owner securely and within milliseconds.

Type of Machine Learning

Computer Vision + Deep Learning (Convolutional Neural Networks – CNNs).

Input Data

Images of the user’s face captured by the front-facing camera (RGB, infrared, or depth, depending on the phone model).

Expected Output

  • Access granted (face matches the registered user).
  • Access denied (unknown or non-matching face).

Real-World Relevance

Enables fast, convenient, and secure unlocking. Used by modern biometric systems such as iPhone FaceID, Samsung Face Unlock, and others.
