Use Case 3: Student Performance Prediction

Problem Description

Educational institutions often struggle to identify students at risk of failing. Without early detection, it is difficult to provide timely academic support.

Type of Machine Learning

This use case uses Supervised Learning, specifically classification, to predict student outcomes.

Input Data

  • Study hours per week
  • Attendance percentage
  • Previous grades
  • Participation level
  • Assignment completion

Expected Output

The model predicts whether the student will pass or fail.

Real-World Relevance

This system allows institutions to identify at-risk students early, improving performance and reducing dropout rates through data-driven decisions.
