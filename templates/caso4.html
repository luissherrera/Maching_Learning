Student Dropout Prediction

Problem

Many students leave their studies without warning, impacting both their personal future and institutional performance.

Machine Learning Type

Supervised Learning — Binary Classification

The model learns from historical student data to predict:
0 = No dropout
1 = Dropout risk

Algorithms

  • Random Forest: High accuracy and robust performance
  • Logistic Regression: Simple, fast, interpretable
  • XGBoost: High efficiency and performance for complex datasets

Input Data

  • Grades and GPA
  • Attendance records
  • Number of failed courses
  • Current semester
  • Study hours per week

Output

0 → No Dropout

1 → Dropout Risk

The model can also provide a probability score for each student.

Impact

  • Enable early intervention for at-risk students
  • Provide better support and guidance
  • Improve overall institutional performance and retention
